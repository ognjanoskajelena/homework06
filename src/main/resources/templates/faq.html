Frequently Asked Questions

You can simply join an initiative by clicking on the button labeled as Join this initiative, which you can find it whenever you open the initiative's details.

The course of an initiative is influenced solely by the participants' actions. I.e., whenever a participant joins an initiative, fills a poll, becomes part of a discussion or attends an event, he/she plays an important role in the initiative's course.

In the initiative's tab labeled as EVENTS you will see the opened events for that initiative. You can announce your presence by simply clicking on the Going button.

Whenever you post a comment on a discussion, it will appear with your username, the content that you wrote and in the top right corner of it, there will be a red trashcan icon. By clicking on that trashcan the comment will be deleted and therefore removed from the discussion.
